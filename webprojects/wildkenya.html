Today's Post :LEOPARDS

Leopards are considered one of the most dangerous animals in kenya.Classified in the BIG FIVE,these animals are cherished and protected in the wildlands.
Leopards are considered dangerous due to:
  • Ability to blend in the sorrounding and approach prey unnoticed,
  • Speed : can reach a top speed of 60km/hr,
  • Mascular & powerful muscles in nature making them suitable to prey on bigger animals(can carry a young giraffe,125kg up 5.7m tree)
Back in 2018,there rare black leopard(Black Panther),Was discoverd in Kenya Laikipia county for the first time in 100yrs.
Since Then there has been 5 Confirmed black leopards in roaming in Laikipia. The sighting of black Leopards Is very rare as the first one took upto 6months to get clear view of this rare animal after it was confirmed to be present in kenya.
